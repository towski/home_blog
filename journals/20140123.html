Last night I started taking unicorn apart in order to add process concurrency for channels. The idea is that you should be able to take a bunch of channels (basically threads) and distribute them easily over a number of processes, sporadically writing back to the master. I am basically trying to increase the concurrency of this video. That shows about 2000 threads hopping around each with some basic AI. That project is called Dwarf.
